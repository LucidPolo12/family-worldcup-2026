❓ How To Use the App

Everything you can do here, in plain English.

📱 Getting in

Open the app on any phone or computer — nothing to install, no password. Tip: add it to your phone's home screen for one-tap access.

✍️ Make Your Picks

Tap Make Your Picks at the top, choose your name, and use the + / − buttons to set the score you think each game will end. Hit Submit ✅ — done.

Changed your mind? Open it again, adjust, and Submit — your newest version always counts. When a game kicks off, that pick locks automatically.

⭐ You don't have to pick every game — pick as many or as few as you like.
🏆 The Leaderboard

Scoring: 3 points for the right result (win, lose or draw), and 8 points for nailing the exact score. Most points wins!

🥇 Champion & Podium picks ⭐ new!

In Make Your Picks, open the 🏆 Champion & Podium tab and call the top four of the whole tournament. Big bonus points for an exact finish: 50 for the Champion, 35 for the Runner-up, 20 for 3rd place, and 10 for 4th — up to 115 in all. You only score for a team in the exact spot, so place them carefully!

All four — Champion, Runner-up, 3rd & 4th — are open to pick and change as often as you like, so you can keep refining your path as teams advance and others fall. They all lock at the first Quarterfinal kickoff (Thu, Jul 9 · 4:00 PM ET).

📅 Today's Games

See today's matches with kickoff times in your local time, plus who's already picked and who still needs to.

🤝 The Pick-Off ⭐ family favorite

The second a game kicks off, everyone's predictions for it are revealed side by side — and after the final whistle you'll see who nailed it. Sealed until kickoff so nobody can peek! Check it during games for instant bragging rights.

🔴 Live during the game ⭐ new!

While a match is being played, its Pick-Off card shows the live score with a 🔴 LIVE clock, and everyone's points update in real time based on the current score. A * means those points are provisional — they can change with the next goal and only lock in at full time. Pull it up while you watch and cheer (or sweat 😅) every goal!

📈 Win Odds ⭐ handy

Every upcoming game shows a colored bar with each team's chance to win, draw or lose — a great hint when you're deciding your score.

⏰ Countdown
